This surface chart shows the species along the surveyed transects of the site's surface. Mean(z_rock_height) is the height of the mean height of the surface above water. Therefore, 0 is sea-level and species that have a negative z are underwater. All of the axes' tickmarks are in meters.


This river chart shows the percentage of species a the site over the surveyed years. You can toggle on and off a species (or multiple) to get a better look at other species.


This pie chart shows the most recently surveyed year's percentages of species at the site. Hover over different colors to display the percentages.


This bar graph shows the percentage of species a the site over the surveyed years. The toggle on the bottom can be adjusted to see more or less years of species at a time.
